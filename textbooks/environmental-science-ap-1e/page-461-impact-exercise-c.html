Chapter 16

Page 461: Exercise c

STEP 1
This question asks us to calculate the tipping fee—the cost of disposing municipal solid waste (MSW) at a landfill—for one household over the course of a year.

STEP 2
We're given the following data:

Household size: 4 people
Daily waste generation per person: 4 pounds
Tipping fee: $60 per ton

STEP 3
First, calculate total annual waste for the household:

Daily total = 4 people × 4 pounds = 16 pounds/day
Annual total = 16 pounds/day × 365 days = 5,840 pounds/year

STEP 4
Convert pounds to tons:

1 ton = 2,000 pounds
5,840 ÷ 2,000 = 2.92 tons/year

STEP 5
Now multiply the total waste in tons by the tipping fee:

2.92 tons × $60/ton = $175.20

Result: Tipping cost for one year = $175.20
