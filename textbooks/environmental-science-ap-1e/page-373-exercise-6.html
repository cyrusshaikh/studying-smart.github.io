Chapter 13

Page 373: Exercise 6

Step 1
Objective:
Determine which statement can be reasonably inferred from the provided graphs.

Step 2
Analysis of the Graphs:
From observing the graphs (as described):

Graph (a): Shows weekly energy output increasing with wind speed.
Graph (b): Compares annual energy output for two turbine models at various wind speeds.

Key conclusions:

  • Energy output increases with wind speed for both models.
  • Model 1 consistently produces more energy than Model 2 at any given wind speed.
  • Model 1 reaches a maximum annual output of around 12,000 kWh.
  • Given its higher efficiency, Model 1 offers greater output and thus better value over time.

Result: (e) Model 1 is more cost-effective.
