Chapter 13

Page 373: Exercise 9

Step 1
Objective:
Identify the accurate conclusion based on the provided diagram showing energy sources used in various sectors of the United States.

Step 2
Analysis of Energy Use by Sector:

  • In the transportation sector, petroleum is the dominant energy source, supplying approximately 96%, with natural gas contributing around 2%.
  • In the industrial sector, petroleum accounts for 44%, coal for 9%, and natural gas for 37%.
  • For electricity generation, coal is the leading source at 51%, followed by natural gas at 17%, and petroleum at only 2%.
  • Across all sectors, fossil fuels—petroleum, coal, and natural gas—are the primary energy sources.

Result: (d)
