Chapter 12, Page 339: Exercise 1

Step 1

This question asks us to identify which option represents a renewable energy resource.

Step 2

Non-renewable energy sources are those that cannot be replenished quickly once they are used. These include fossil fuels such as coal, oil, and natural gas, as well as nuclear energy. These resources take millions of years to form and are depleted much faster than they can regenerate.

Step 3

In contrast, renewable energy sources naturally replenish over short periods. Among the choices, wind energy is renewable—it is continuously available and doesn’t run out after use.

Result
(d)
