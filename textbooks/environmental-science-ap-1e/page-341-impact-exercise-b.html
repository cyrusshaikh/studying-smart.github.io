Chapter 12

Page 341: Exercise b

Step 1
We're tasked with calculating and comparing the cumulative cost and carbon dioxide emissions of two car types over a ten-year period.

Step 2
Based on earlier calculations:

Compact Sedan:
• Purchase Price: $15,000
• Annual Fuel: 600 gallons
• Fuel Cost: $1,800/year
• CO₂: 12,000 lbs/year
• First-Year Total: $16,800

Hybrid Car:
• Purchase Price: $22,000
• Annual Fuel: 240 gallons
• Fuel Cost: $720/year
• CO₂: 4,800 lbs/year
• First-Year Total: $22,720
