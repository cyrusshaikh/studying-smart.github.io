Chapter 11

Page 309: Exercise A

STEP 1
This question asks us to determine the ecological footprint for various food items based on the annual amount consumed by an individual. The footprint is measured in hectares per year.

STEP 2
To calculate the ecological footprint for each food item, we use the formula:

Ecological Footprint = (Land Required for 1000 kg ÷ 1000) × Amount Consumed
Let’s walk through an example using beef:

Annual beef consumption per person = 43 kg
Land required to produce 1000 kg of beef = 15.7 hectares
Footprint = (15.7 ÷ 1000) × 43 = 0.6751 hectares/year
