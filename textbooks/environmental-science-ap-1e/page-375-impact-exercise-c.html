Chapter 13

Page 375: Exercise c

STEP 1
This question asks us to determine which type of light bulb has a smaller ecological footprint.

STEP 2
An ecological footprint refers to the measure of how much natural resources humans consume and how much waste they produce in the process of sustaining their activities.

STEP 3
In the United States, a large portion of electricity is generated through the burning of fossil fuels, which releases pollutants that contribute to air pollution and environmental degradation. Since compact fluorescent bulbs (CFLs) use significantly less electricity than incandescent bulbs for the same output, they lead to lower fossil fuel consumption and fewer emissions.

From an environmental perspective, compact fluorescent bulbs leave a smaller ecological footprint due to their energy efficiency and reduced environmental impact.

Result: Compact fluorescent bulb
