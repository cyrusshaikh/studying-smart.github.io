STEP 1
Earthquakes are caused by the movement of tectonic plates, such as when two plates collide or when one plate is subducted beneath another. These interactions create intense pressure and stress within the Earth's crust, which is suddenly released as seismic energy, resulting in a powerful and often destructive earthquake.

STEP 2
The epicenter is the point on the Earth’s surface that lies directly above the focus (or hypocenter), which is the location within the Earth where the earthquake originates. The strongest shaking is typically felt at or near the epicenter.
