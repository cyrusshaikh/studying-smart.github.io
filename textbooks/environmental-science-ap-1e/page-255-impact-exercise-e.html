Chapter 9

Page 255: Exercise E

STEP 1
Given that one person saves 33,945 liters of water annually and water costs $1 per 1,000 liters, we convert liters to tons (1 ton = 1,000 L):
33,945 ÷ 1,000 = 33.9 tons of water
So, the yearly savings for one person is:
33.9 × $1 = $33.90

STEP 2
For a household of 4 people with similar usage, the total savings is:
4 × $33.90 = $135.60 per year

Result: $135.60 saved annually per household
