Page 23 – Exercise 6

Free-Response Question

Step 1

Anthropogenic activities refer to actions caused by humans, such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation for agriculture, overexploitation of natural resources, and air pollution from oil combustion. These are all human-driven processes, unlike naturally occurring events that happen without any human involvement.

Step 2

While human actions can sometimes lead to forest fires, many wildfires are triggered by natural causes. For example, during intense storms, lightning strikes can ignite trees, and with the help of strong winds, the fire can rapidly spread across the forest.
