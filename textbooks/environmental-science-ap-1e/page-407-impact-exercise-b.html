Chapter 14

Page 407: Exercise b

STEP 1
This question asks us to explain how to reduce the death rate among children under five years of age due to diarrheal diseases.

STEP 2
Diarrheal diseases are caused by pathogens such as viruses, bacteria, and parasites. These organisms often contaminate drinking water and food through exposure to untreated human or animal waste.

STEP 3
Key Methods to Reduce Child Mortality from Diarrhea

Improve Sanitation Infrastructure:
- Build and maintain effective sanitation systems, especially in rural and peri-urban areas.
- Implement proper solid waste disposal to prevent fecal contamination of water sources.

Ensure Access to Safe Drinking Water:
- Promote the use of filtered, boiled, or chemically treated water.
- Invest in community water purification systems.

Promote Hygiene Education:
- Teach handwashing with soap, especially after defecation and before eating.
- Educate families about hygienic food storage and preparation.

Vaccinate Against Diarrheal Pathogens:
- Administer rotavirus and similar vaccines to infants.

Encourage Exclusive Breastfeeding:
- Breastfeeding provides immunity and protects infants from unsafe water and food.

Result: Improve sanitation, clean water access, hygiene, vaccination, breastfeeding
