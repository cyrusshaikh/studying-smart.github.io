Chapter 12

Page 341: Exercise d

Step 1
Objective:
We are asked to suggest practical strategies for lowering carbon dioxide (CO₂) emissions from a compact sedan.

Step 2
Effective Ways to Reduce CO₂ Emissions from a Compact Sedan:

Switch to Cleaner Fuels and Improve Driving Habits:
Use lower-emission fuels such as ethanol blends or reformulated gasoline when available. Practice smooth driving by avoiding hard acceleration, maintaining steady speeds, and minimizing idling to reduce fuel consumption and emissions.

Keep the Vehicle Well-Maintained:
Routine engine tune-ups, replacing air filters, and keeping the fuel system clean help the car burn fuel more efficiently. Ensuring that emission control devices like oxygen sensors are working can also reduce pollutants.

Reduce Personal Car Use When Possible:
Utilize public transportation, walk, bike, or carpool to decrease fuel usage and carbon output. Even modest reductions in personal vehicle use can lead to significant environmental benefits over time.

Final Answer:
A combination of fuel choice, vehicle maintenance, and alternative transportation can meaningfully reduce CO₂ emissions from compact sedans.
