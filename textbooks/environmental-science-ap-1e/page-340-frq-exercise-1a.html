Chapter 12

Page 340: Exercise 1a

Step 1
We are asked to determine the total cost of owning and operating a mini refrigerator over a 5-year period. The following information is provided:

Purchase cost of the refrigerator = $100
Power consumption = 100 watts
Operating time = 12 hours/day
Lifespan = 5 years
Electricity rate = $0.10 per kWh

We begin by calculating the total energy the refrigerator uses in five years.

Step 2
Daily energy consumption: 100 watts × 12 hours = 1200 watt-hours/day = 1.2 kWh/day
Annual energy consumption: 1.2 kWh/day × 365 days/year = 438 kWh/year
Total energy used over 5 years: 438 kWh/year × 5 = 2190 kWh

Step 3
Electricity cost over 5 years: 2190 kWh × $0.10/kWh = $219
Total cost (purchase + electricity): $100 + $219 = $319

Final Answer:
The total cost of owning and operating the refrigerator over five years is $319.
