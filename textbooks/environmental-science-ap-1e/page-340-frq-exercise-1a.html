Page 340: Exercise 1a

Step 1

We are asked to calculate the total cost of owning and operating a mini refrigerator over its expected lifespan.

From the information provided:
Purchase cost of fridge = $100
Power consumption = 100 watts
Daily usage = 12 hours
Expected lifespan = 5 years
Electricity rate = $0.10 per kWh

The first step is to determine the total energy the fridge will use over the 5-year period.

Step 2

To find energy used daily:
Daily energy consumption = 100 W × 12 hr = 1200 W-hr
Convert to kilowatt-hours:
1200 W-hr = 1.2 kWh/day
Now calculate the total energy used over 5 years:
1.2 kWh/day × 365 days/year × 5 years = 2190 kWh

Step 3

Now we calculate the operating cost for electricity:
2190 kWh × $0.10/kWh = $219
Finally, the total cost of ownership is the sum of the purchase price and electricity usage:
$100 + $219 = $319

Result: The total cost of owning and operating the refrigerator for 5 years is $319.
