Chapter 12

Page 339: Exercise 3

STEP 1
This question requires us to determine which mode of transportation is the most fuel-efficient based on the energy consumed per person per kilometer.

STEP 2
Referring to Table 12.1, the energy consumption values are as follows:

  • Air travel: 2.1 × 10⁶ joules/person-km
  • Bus: 1.7 × 10⁶ joules/person-km
  • Car (1 person): 3.6 × 10⁶ joules/person-km
  • Car (3 people): 1.2 × 10⁶ joules/person-km
  • Train: 1.1 × 10⁶ joules/person-km

When comparing these values, the train uses the least amount of energy per passenger, making it the most fuel-efficient option.

Result: (a)
