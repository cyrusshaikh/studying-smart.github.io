Exercise 18

In this problem, we are asked to describe the center and spread of two different distributions based on their histograms.

First Lightning Flash Times:

The histogram for lightning flash times is approximately symmetric, with the tallest bars near the center. The center is around 12 hours after midnight. The data values range from about 7 hours after midnight to 17 hours after midnight.

Shakespeare's Word Lengths:

The histogram for word lengths is skewed to the right, with the highest bars positioned on the left and a tail stretching to the right. The center is around 5 letters. The spread of the data extends from 1 letter to 12 letters.

First Lightning Flash Times: Center ≈ 12 hours after midnight; Spread: 7 to 17 hours after midnight

Shakespeare's Word Lengths: Center ≈ 5 letters; Spread: 1 to 12 letters
