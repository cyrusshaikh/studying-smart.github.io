Distribution Description

Shape: The distribution is bimodal since there are two clusters of data points, one around 24 mpg and another around 28 mpg.

Center: The center appears to be approximately 26 mpg, as this value lies roughly between the two peaks.

Spread: The data values extend from 21 mpg to 32 mpg.

Outliers: The values at 21 and 32 mpg are separated from the majority of the data and could be considered potential outliers.
