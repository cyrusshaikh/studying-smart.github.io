Description of the Distribution:

Most of the data values lie near the top of both stemplots, with tails of more unusual data values near the bottom. This indicates that the distribution is skewed right.

Since the distribution is skewed right, the center lies slightly below the peak, at about $35.

The data values range from $3 to $93.

There are gaps in the stemplot with split stems, suggesting potential outliers, although the gaps are not present in the stemplot without split stems.

Interpretation:

The amount spent by shoppers at this supermarket ranges from $3 to $93. On average, shoppers spend about $35, with most spending smaller amounts and a few spending much more.
