Exercise 5b Solution

Problem

We are given the percentages of females earning doctorates in various fields of study. We need to determine whether a pie chart is an appropriate way to graph this data given in excercise 5a.

Solution

A pie chart works best when the total of all categories equals 100%, since each slice represents a proportion of the whole.

Let's add up the percentages from the provided data:

15.4% (Computer Science) + 60.8% (Education) + 11.1% (Engineering) + 40.7% (Life Sciences) + 21.7% (Physical Sciences) + 62.2% (Psychology) = 211.9%

Since the total percentage adds up to 211.9%, which is far greater than 100%, this data cannot be represented accurately with a pie chart.

Final Answer: No
