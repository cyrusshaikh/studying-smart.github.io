Exercise 24c

In this problem, we are asked to select the most appropriate graph to display data on students' most popular radio stations.

Appropriate Graph Selection:

Since radio station names are non-numeric categories, this is a categorical variable. For such data, bar graphs and pie charts are typically used. However, because there may be many radio stations, a bar graph is the better choice, as pie charts become difficult to interpret when too many categories exist.

Final Answer: Bar Graph
