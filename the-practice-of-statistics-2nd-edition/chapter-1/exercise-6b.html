Exercise 6b

We are provided with the same data from Exercise 6a. This time, we are asked to display the data using a bar graph, including a category for "Other" causes.

Data Table

Cause of Death Number of Deaths
Motor vehicle accident 42,340
Fall 11,858

Drowning 4,051
Fire 3,601
Other 20,340
Total 92,353

Calculating "Other" Causes

To find the number of deaths from unlisted causes, we subtract the total deaths from the sum of the known causes:

92,353 - (42,340 + 11,858 + 10,163 + 4,051 + 3,601) = 20,340
