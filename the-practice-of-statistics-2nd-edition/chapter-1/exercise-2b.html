We are given the variable Age (in years). The task is to classify whether this variable is categorical or quantitative.

A categorical variable divides individuals into distinct groups or labels that do not involve numbers. In contrast, a quantitative variable represents numerical values that can be measured or calculated, such as height, weight, or age.

Since age is measured in years and takes on numerical values (for example, 18 years, 42 years, etc.), it is a quantitative variable.

Final Answer: Quantitative
