Distribution Description

Center: The most frequently occurring value is 35, which is used to estimate the center of the distribution.

Spread: The data values range from a minimum of 14 to a maximum of 54.

Shape: The distribution is approximately symmetric, as most data values are clustered around the center without a noticeable skew.
