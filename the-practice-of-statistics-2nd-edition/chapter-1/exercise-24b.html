Exercise 24b

In this problem, we are asked to determine the most appropriate graphs to represent both the number of hours studied and how study hours change across semesters.

Number of Hours Studied:

This is a quantitative variable that can take on both integer and decimal values. Since decimal values are included, stemplots and dotplots are not ideal. Ogives are typically used for cumulative percentages, which are not required here. A histogram is the best choice to represent the distribution of study hours.

Change in Study Hours Across Semesters:

Because this variable tracks how study hours vary over different semesters (a time component), a time plot is the most appropriate graph to visualize the changes across periods.

Number of Hours Studied: Histogram
Change in Study Hours Over Semesters: Time Plot
