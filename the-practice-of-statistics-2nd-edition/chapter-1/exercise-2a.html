We are given the variable Gender, which includes the values "male" and "female." Our task is to classify this variable as either categorical or quantitative.

A categorical variable places individuals into specific groups or categories, such as names, labels, or groups that do not involve numbers. On the other hand, a quantitative variable deals with numerical values that can be measured or counted.

Since gender divides individuals into groups (male or female), and these values are not numerical measurements, this variable is classified as categorical.

Final Answer: Categorical
