In this problem, we are asked to estimate the center of the distribution using the ogive (cumulative frequency graph).

Center Estimation:

The center of a distribution corresponds approximately to the 50th percentile, or a cumulative relative frequency of 50%.

By examining the ogive, we see that when the cumulative frequency reaches 50%, the corresponding value on the horizontal axis is around $28. Therefore, we estimate the center to be approximately $28.

Final Answer: $28
