The variable being analyzed here is Smoker, which indicates whether an individual smokes or not, with answers being either "Yes" or "No."

Variables are classified into two main types:

  • Categorical variables assign individuals to groups or labels, such as colors or yes/no responses, without involving measurements or calculations.
  • Quantitative variables involve numbers that represent measured amounts or counts.

Since the options for Smoker are simply "Yes" or "No," this variable places people into categories rather than assigning any numerical value. Therefore, it is a categorical variable.

Final Answer: Categorical
