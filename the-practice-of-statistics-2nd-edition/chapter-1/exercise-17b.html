Exercise 17b

In this problem, we are asked to estimate the percentage of years that had no days in April with temperatures below zero.

Calculation:

The bar corresponding to 0 frost days has a height of 15, meaning that in 15 out of the 65 years, there were no days in April where the temperature fell below zero.

To find the percentage, we calculate:

15 ÷ 65 ≈ 0.2308

Converting this to a percentage: 0.2308 × 100 ≈ 23.08%

Final Answer: 23.08%
