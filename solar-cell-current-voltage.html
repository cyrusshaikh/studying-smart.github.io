Current and Voltage

Question: The Current and Voltage (potential) of the dye sensitized solar cell was measured. Why are these parameters useful when assessing a solar cell? Explain.
Current and voltage are key indicators of how well a solar cell is working. The current tells us how many electrons are flowing through the circuit—basically, how much electric charge is being generated. The voltage shows the electrical "push" or potential energy that moves those electrons. When we measure both, we get a complete picture of the solar cell’s ability to generate power. This helps us understand how efficient the cell is and how it performs under different environmental conditions like light intensity or temperature.
